01

Your Situation & Challenges

Breaking Through Entrenched CPQ Incumbents

Your core market challenge is one of the toughest in B2B SaaS: most of your prospects are either running a homegrown CPQ solution they built themselves, or locked into Oracle or Salesforce CPQ. Both groups share a common mindset - switching feels risky, expensive, and disruptive. The real opportunity is to identify and prioritize accounts that are already questioning their current solution - teams frustrated by slow update cycles, limited flexibility, or the months-long wait for a new quote configuration.

Scaling Outreach Beyond Your In-House Playbook

You already have your ICPs defined and are building target company lists - which puts you ahead of most early-stage companies. The challenge now is volume, speed, and consistency. Running personalized multi-channel outreach at scale requires either significant internal bandwidth or a dedicated partner. Adding FORECOM as a complement means your team focuses on closing, while outreach runs in parallel at full capacity.

Reaching the Right People at the Right Moment

In CPQ, timing is everything. A prospect posting a job for a CPQ Admin or researching "Salesforce CPQ vs alternatives" is a warm signal that they are evaluating options. Without an intent data layer and a systematic way to capture these signals, your outreach is largely cold - hitting the right company but at the wrong moment. Building a signal-aware targeting strategy from the start dramatically improves response rates and pipeline quality.

Building Brand Awareness Before the Ask

SalesRun is an early-stage company entering a market where Oracle and Salesforce are household names. Even decision-makers who would benefit from SalesRun's flexibility may have never heard of you. Outreach to a completely cold audience with no prior brand exposure yields lower response rates. A targeted LinkedIn advertising layer that warms up your target accounts before your SDRs reach out can meaningfully improve conversion at every stage of the funnel.

Hitting 5 Clients This Year Ahead of Your Funding Round

You are targeting 5 new clients in 2026 as you enter your second funding round - a concrete, time-bound goal. Your pipeline needs to be moving now, not in three months. The priority is maximizing the number of qualified sales calls your team is having each week. Every week without a structured, high-volume outreach engine running is a week of lost pipeline momentum.

We handle everything from initial setup through ongoing management: writing and A/B testing messaging, expanding your LinkedIn network, managing inbound replies, and tracking buying signals from your target accounts. Your sales team receives a pipeline of leads ready for a discovery call.

  • Up to 2,000 contacts reached per month, up to 5 sales reps connected
  • Continuous contact database build and updates
  • Full messaging preparation, personalization and campaign management
  • LinkedIn network expansion and signal monitoring (15 signals)

Option B - Outreach + ABM

Outreach Combined with Account-Based Marketing

For SalesRun's goal of maximizing sales calls while building brand awareness in a skeptical market, we recommend combining the Outreach Full-Service layer with a structured ABM program on LinkedIn. This is particularly effective for CPQ - a category where prospects need to trust the vendor before they will even take a call.

The ABM layer works in parallel with outreach: we identify your highest-priority target accounts, upload them to LinkedIn Ads, and serve them a sequence of targeted content - TOFU awareness about the cost of legacy CPQ, MOFU comparison content positioning SalesRun against Salesforce/Oracle, and BOFU demo-request ads for the most engaged accounts. We also set up competitor content comparison pages to capture prospects actively searching for Salesforce CPQ alternatives - creating a first-party intent signal before they fill in any form.

  • Everything in Option A, plus full LinkedIn Ads management (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU)
  • ABM account scoring and segmentation via ZenABM + Salesforce sync
  • Competitor comparison content campaigns and intent data generation
  • Account warming sequences before direct outreach for higher response rates
  • ABM-specific reporting and optimization
  • Marketing ad spend and additional tool costs (HubSpot/ZenABM) paid by client separately

Option B Add-On: ABM GTM Streams

Stream A - Outreach Sequences

Persona-Based Outreach to Target Accounts

We build segmented contact lists from your ICP account pool and run personalized outreach sequences to decision-makers - VP Sales, RevOps, Sales Ops. Contacts are segmented by relationship level: high-affinity accounts receive manual, highly personalized outreach; broader lists go through automated sequences. Messaging is anchored to the "replace the incumbent" narrative - why SalesRun over a homegrown solution or Salesforce CPQ.

  • Account and contact list build (Clay, BuiltWith, Apollo)
  • Sequence copywriting per persona and segment
  • Manual outreach for high-priority accounts, automated for broader lists
  • Weekly iteration cycles based on reply signals
Stream B - Competitor & Alternative ABM

Targeting Companies on Competing or Legacy CPQ Solutions

We identify companies currently using competitor or legacy CPQ solutions - via competitor case studies, BuiltWith data, and custom Clay scrapers - and run targeted LinkedIn Ads and outreach sequences specifically to this segment. Accounts are scored based on ad engagement and moved through the funnel from awareness to direct outreach. This is the highest-intent segment: companies that already have a CPQ, feel the pain, and are actively evaluating alternatives.

  • Account lists sourced from competitor websites, case studies, and tech detection tools
  • LinkedIn Ads targeting this segment with comparison and replacement content
  • Account engagement scoring - most engaged accounts escalated to direct outreach
  • Outreach sequences anchored to competitor pain points
Stream C - Inbound & Search Intent

Capturing In-Market Demand via Search & Retargeting

We run Google and Microsoft Search campaigns targeting high-value keywords - primarily competitor terms ("Salesforce CPQ alternative", "Oracle CPQ replacement") and bottom-funnel CPQ evaluation queries. Traffic is driven to dedicated competitor comparison landing pages. Visitors who don't convert are added to LinkedIn retargeting audiences and outreach lists, generating a first-party intent signal: we know which companies visited, what they searched for, and can prioritize outreach accordingly.

  • Keyword analysis and search campaign setup (Google/Microsoft Ads)
  • Competitor alternative and comparison landing pages
  • LinkedIn retargeting of website visitors from search
  • Intent-based outreach list generation from search traffic
