The Problem

Warm leads are going cold
in your inbox

Your Smartlead campaign runs across 55 email accounts and generates consistent replies. But handling them manually — from a single forwarded inbox — isn't sustainable.

  • Every reply gets forwarded to one inbox — volume is hard to triage manually at scale
  • Not every reply deserves the same energy — but you can't tell at a glance without reading them all
  • Delayed responses signal disinterest to warm prospects — reply speed directly impacts conversion
  • Replying from the Smartlead master inbox breaks your personal outreach feel
  • Hours spent on "not relevant" replies that could be handled automatically

The Solution

Three categories.
One automated pipeline.

The moment a reply lands in Smartlead, an AI reads the full thread, classifies the intent, and takes the right action — instantly.

Category A

Not Relevant

End users only, wrong person, unsubscribe requests, out-of-office replies

Auto-reply sent immediately. Polite, professional close. Lead removed from follow-up. You never see it.

Category B

Somewhat Relevant

Curious but unclear role, uses NTRIP internally, wants more information about Glopos

Auto-reply sent immediately. Light Glopos context, soft CTA. Keeps the door open without your effort.

Category C

Priority

Runs an RTK corrections service, operates a caster, wants to book a call, asks about Glopos features or migration

Draft created in your inbox. You receive a notification. Review 5 lines, hit Send. Done in 30 seconds.

Your Experience

What this looks like
for you, Tim

After a one-time setup, your daily interaction with incoming replies changes completely.

Categories A + B · Automatic

You do nothing.

The AI reads the reply, classifies it, and sends the appropriate response — all within seconds. No action needed from you. The reply goes out from your email, in your name, in your tone.

You can optionally review a weekly digest of what was sent, but there's no obligation to act on it.

Category C · 30 seconds

You get a notification.

When a high-priority reply arrives, you receive an email:

"Priority reply from Jan Novák at CzechGNSS — draft ready in your inbox."

You open your email, read the prepared draft, make any edits you want, and hit Send. That's it. The AI has already done the work of writing a personalized, context-aware reply.

How We Start

Trial period — all drafts,
zero auto-sends

Before the system sends anything automatically, we run a controlled trial. Every reply — across all three categories — is prepared as a draft first. You review, approve, and we calibrate.

Days 1–30 · Trial Mode

Everything goes to drafts.

During the trial, no reply is sent automatically. Every classified message — A, B, or C — lands in your inbox as a prepared draft. You see exactly how the AI would respond, in your voice, for every type of reply.

You review, send what looks good, and flag anything that needs adjusting. We use that feedback to fine-tune the classification and reply quality in real-time.

After Trial · Live Mode

Switch to auto — on your terms.

Once you're confident in the output quality, we flip categories A and B to auto-send. Category C always stays as a draft for your approval — that never changes.

You decide when you're ready. There's no pressure to switch before you're comfortable. And if you want to keep categories A or B in draft mode longer, that's completely fine too.

Trial length is flexible — 14 days if the volume is high and you feel ready, or a full 30 days at a more relaxed pace. Your call.
