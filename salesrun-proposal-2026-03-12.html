Client Situation & Challenges

Early-Stage with No Deployed Lead Generation Yet

SalesRun has just finalized its core messaging and values - a critical milestone. However, you have not yet deployed a full go-to-market strategy, and time is working against you: your goal is to close 5 clients this year as you enter your second funding round. Every month without a structured lead generation engine is a month of missed pipeline opportunity.

A Competitive, Entrenched CPQ Market

Most companies in your target segments either rely on homegrown CPQ tools or are locked into Oracle and Salesforce CPQ. These prospects are not actively looking to switch - they are anchored to existing systems. The real opportunity lies in the minority who are in-market right now: companies frustrated with outdated tools, facing scaling challenges, or actively evaluating alternatives.

Low Brand Awareness Among Target Accounts

As a new market entrant, SalesRun is largely unknown to your ICP. Cold outreach to companies that have never encountered your brand faces natural resistance. Without prior brand exposure, even a well-crafted email lands in an unfamiliar inbox - reducing response rates and elongating the sales cycle. This is particularly true when competing against recognized names like Salesforce CPQ.

In-House Playbook Needs External Scale

You already have an internal lead generation playbook and an in-house sales team ready to run demos. What you need is not a replacement for what you have built - it is complementary capacity: additional prospecting reach, intent signal layering, and outreach infrastructure that multiplies your team's output without adding internal headcount.

02

Proposed Solution

Cold Email Outreach

Full-Service Outreach Infrastructure

Because you need pipeline fast and have a defined ICP, we build and operate a complete cold email outreach system - from technical deliverability setup to targeting, copywriting, A/B testing, and ongoing optimization. Your sales team receives warm, qualified leads handed directly into Salesforce; they only need to run the demos.

  • Email infrastructure setup (dedicated domains, warmup, deliverability audits)
  • Contact database building and enrichment aligned to your ICP
  • Messaging strategy and A/B variant testing
  • Ongoing campaign management, monitoring, and optimization
  • Direct Salesforce CRM integration for clean lead handoff
Account-Based Marketing (ABM)

Brand Warm-Up + Targeted Outreach on LinkedIn

Because cold outreach to an unknown brand underperforms, we layer a LinkedIn ABM campaign on top of your target account list. We run sponsored content toward your exact ICP personas at the accounts you care about - building brand recognition before the first email arrives. We then prioritize outreach to the accounts showing the highest engagement, dramatically improving response rates from your most valuable targets.

  • LinkedIn ad setup: copy, creative, and audience targeting by company list and persona
  • Engagement signal monitoring to identify highest-intent accounts
  • Outreach prioritization based on ad engagement data
  • Coordinated sales and marketing messaging across channels
Intent Data & Signal-Based Selling

Reach Companies Actively Looking to Switch

Because most CPQ market share is locked up in Oracle and Salesforce, we identify companies showing in-market signals right now. This includes custom intent scrapers monitoring job postings (companies hiring for CPQ or RevOps roles), competitor comparison content campaigns driving search traffic to SalesRun landing pages, and third-party intent signals from tools like Apollo. Outreach effort is concentrated on prospects with the highest propensity to buy.

  • Competitor content landing pages (SalesRun vs. Salesforce CPQ, vs. Oracle)
  • Custom intent scrapers based on job posting and hiring signals
  • Third-party intent data integration (Apollo or equivalent)
  • In-market company prioritization feeding directly into outreach queues
CRM & RevOps

Salesforce Optimization for Outbound Sales

Because you are already on Salesforce, we optimize it to support the outbound motion - ensuring all outreach and ABM activity flows cleanly into your pipeline, your sales reps receive timely notifications for booked demos, and leadership has clear visibility into campaign performance and conversion rates.

  • Outbound pipeline stage setup and automated lead routing
  • Sales rep notifications and calendar booking flow
  • Campaign attribution and performance dashboards
